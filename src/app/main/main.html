Seminole Springs Antique Rose & Herb Farm

We are a family run nursery specializing in an extensive variety of potted herbs, old garden roses, perennials and butterfly attracting plants available online and at our farm.antique heirloom roses. Our gift shed boasts herbal crafts, dried culinary herbs, herbal teas, essential oils, and novelties for the gardener. Group tours with a themed program are popular, as we are able to accommodate 50, or comfortably seat 30 for a picnic style lunch in our pavilion. Thank you for visiting our site. We are open year round Thursday through Saturday from 10 to 4, so stop in to share our love of gardening and we'll talk roses and herbs.
